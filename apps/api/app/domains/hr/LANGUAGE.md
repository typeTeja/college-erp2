# People & HR Domain - Ubiquitous Language

## Core Entities

- **Staff**: Any non-teaching employee of the institution (Administrative, Technical, Supporting).
- **Faculty**: Academic staff involved in teaching and research (Professors, Lecturers).
- **Designation**: The official job title or position held by an employee (e.g., "Senior Professor", "System Administrator").
- **Department**: The organizational unit an employee belongs to (inherited from shared/identity).
- **Payroll**: (Future) The financial compensation record for employees.
- **Attendance**: (Future/Integrated) Record of employee presence/absence.

## Key Actions

- **Appointment**: The process of onboarding a new employee.
- **Assignment**: Linking an employee to a department or role.
- **Termination/Resignation**: The process of an employee leaving the service.
 attendance tracking and leave management are part of the broader HR lifecycle.
