# Hostel Domain Ubiquitous Language

- **Residency**: The state of a student living in the hostel.
- **BedAssignment**: (Allocation) The specific bed assigned to a resident.
- **OccupancyState**: (Status) Whether a room or bed is occupied, vacant, or under maintenance.
- **GatePass**: Permission for a student to leave the campus for a specific duration.
- **Complaint**: A request for maintenance or service within the hostel premises.
