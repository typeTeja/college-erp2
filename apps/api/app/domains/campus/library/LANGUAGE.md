# Library Domain Ubiquitous Language

- **Circulation**: The process of lending and returning library resources.
- **IntellectualResource**: (Book/Media) Any item that can be borrowed or accessed in the library.
- **Patron**: (Member) A student, faculty, or staff member registered with the library.
- **Loan**: (Issue) The act of lending a resource to a patron.
- **OverdueFine**: Fees accrued when a resource is not returned by the due date.
