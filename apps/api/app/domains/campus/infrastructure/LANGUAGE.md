# Infrastructure Domain Ubiquitous Language

- **Facility**: A physical structure like a Building, Wing, or Classroom.
- **MaintenanceLifecycle**: The sequence of events from facility acquisition to decommissioning.
- **ServiceRequest**: (Complaint) A formal request for a facility-related service.
- **AMC**: Annual Maintenance Contract for facilities or large equipment.
- **SpaceUtilization**: The tracking of how facilities are used (e.g., Classroom capacity).
