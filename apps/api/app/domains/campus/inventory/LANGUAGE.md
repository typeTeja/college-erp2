# Inventory Domain Ubiquitous Language

- **PhysicalAsset**: (Asset) A tangible item owned by the institution.
- **CommonStock**: (Stock) Bulk items like stationery or uniforms.
- **SKU**: (Identity) The unique identifier for an asset type.
- **Audit**: (VarianceCheck) The process of verifying physical stock against records.
- **Issuance**: (Allocation) Providing an asset to a person or department.
