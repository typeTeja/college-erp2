# Transport Domain Ubiquitous Language

- **Fleet**: The collection of vehicles operated by the institution.
- **Logistics**: The planning and management of routes and schedules.
- **CommuteAllocation**: (RouteAssignment) Assigning a student/staff to a specific route and vehicle.
- **TripLog**: A record of a specific journey made by a vehicle.
- **FleetStatus**: (Maintenance/Active) The operational state of a vehicle.
