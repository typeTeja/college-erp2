# Communication Domain - Ubiquitous Language

## Core Entities

- **Circular**: An official announcement or notice distributed to staff, faculty, or students.
- **Notification**: A system-generated or user-initiated message sent to specific users or roles.
- **NotificationLog**: A record of sent notifications, including status (Sent, Failed, Read).
- **Template**: Pre-defined content for emails or SMS (moved from settings).

## Key Actions

- **Broadcast**: Sending a circular or notification to a large group.
- **Notify**: Sending a targeted message to an individual or small group.
- **Log**: Recording the delivery and interaction status of communications.
